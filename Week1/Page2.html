Valentino Rossi


Here's some fun facts about Valentino

  • He has the longest winning career in the history of motorcycle racing
  • His nickname is "The Doctor" because it's a title Italians hold great respect for, and is given to someone deserving of the title, not necessarily just the medical profession.
  • He was the oldest racer still competing in the premier class, retiring at 42 years old
