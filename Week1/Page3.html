Marc Marquez

Here's some fun facts about Marc

  1. He is the youngest racer to win a premier class race, at 20 years old
  2. His nickname "The Ant" is because of his relative height, being shorter than most premier class racers
  3. He has a distinctly aggressive riding style, which many believe will be his downfall after so many injuries
